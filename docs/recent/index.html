9:30 am
The Senate will convene at 9:30 AM and will proceed to an Executive Session to resume consideration of Cal. #18, Eric Murphy to be U.S. Circuit Judge for the Sixth Circuit. At 12:30 PM, all post-cloture time will be considered expired, and the Senate will vote on confirmation of the Murphy nomination. The Senate will then proceed to Cal. #11, John Fleming to be Assistant Secretary of Commerce for Economic Development, and the cloture motion will be withdrawn. At 1:45 PM, the Senate will vote on confirmation of the Fleming nomination.
