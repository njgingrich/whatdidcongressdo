The Senate will convene at 10:00 AM. Following Leader remarks, the Senate will proceed to a Legislative Session and resume consideration of Motion to Proceed to H.R. 1865 (Allow States and Victims to Fight Online Sex Trafficking Act of 2017). Senator Sanders or his designee will be recognized to offer a motion to discharge S.J. Res. 54 (A joint resolution to direct the removal of United States Armed Forces from hostilities in the Republic of Yemen that have not been authorized by Congress). There will be up to four hours of debate equally divided, and following the use or yielding back of that time, the Senate will vote in relation to the motion to discharge (approx. 4:15 PM). The Senate will recess from 12:30 to 2:15 PM for the weekly party caucus lunches.
