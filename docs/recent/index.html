The Senate will convene at 10:00 on Tuesday, August 21st, 2018. Following Leader remarks, the Senate will resume consideration of H.R. 6157 (Defense-Labor-HHS-Education Appropriations Minibus).

      The Senate will recess following the disposition of the Kennedy amendment until 2:15 PM for the weekly caucus party lunches.
        9:30 am
        The Senate convened and proceeded to Leader remarks.
          9:30 am
          The Senate proceeded to a Legislative Session.
          • H.R. 6157 (Defense-Labor-HHS-Education Appropriations Minibus).
          • Nelson amend #3733 (To require a Comptroller General of the United States to report on the implementation of the Military Health System Genesis electronic health record). Yeas and nays ordered.
          • Shelby amend #3695 (Substitute).
          • Nelson amend #3733 (To require a Comptroller General of the United States to report on the implementation of the Military Health System Genesis electronic health record). Yeas and nays ordered. The amendment is agreed to by a vote of 95-0.
          • Kennedy amend #3703 (To increase funding for the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline). Yeas and nays ordered. The amendment is agreed to by a vote of 95-0.
