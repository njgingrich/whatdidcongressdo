The Senate will convene at 10:00 AM. Following Leader remarks, will proceed to an Executive Session to resume consideration of Cal. #892, Andrew S. Oldham to be United States Circuit Judge for the Fifth Circuit. At 2:00 PM, the Senate will proceed to two roll call votes:
    11:00 am
    The Senate convened and proceeded to Leader remarks.
    • Cal. #892, Andrew S. Oldham to be United States Circuit Judge for the Fifth Circuit.
    2:30 pm
    The Senate proceeded to an Executive Session.
    • Cal. #892, Andrew S. Oldham to be United States Circuit Judge for the Fifth Circuit. Yeas and nays ordered. The nomination is confirmed by a vote of 50-49.
    • Cal. #903, Ryan Wesley Bounds to be United States Circuit Judge for the Ninth Circuit. Motion to Invoke Cloture. Yeas and nays ordered. The Motion to Invoke Cloture was agreed to by a vote of 50-49.
    • Cal. #903, Ryan Wesley Bounds to be United States Circuit Judge for the Ninth Circuit.
    7:00 pm
    The Senate proceeded to a Period of Morning Business.
    • Cal. #942, Read Adm. (lh) Andrew S. McKinley, to be Rear Admiral. The nomination was confirmed by Unanimous Consent.
    7:00 pm
    The Senate proceeded to a Legislative Session.
    • S. 526 (Microloan Modernization Act of 2018). The Committee-Reported substitute amendment was agreed, the Risch amendment was agreed to, and the bill as amended was passed by Voice Vote.
    • S. 791 (A bill to amend the Small Business Act to expand intellectual property, education and training for small businesses, and for other purposes). The bill was passed by Voice Vote.
    • S. 2850 (A bill to amend the White Mountain Apache Tribe Water Rights Quantification Act of 2010 to clarify the use of amounts in the WMAT Settlement Fund). The Flake amendment was agreed to by Unanimous Consent. The bill as amended was passed by Unanimous Consent.
