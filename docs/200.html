10:00 am
The Senate will convene at 3:00 PM and will proceed to Leader remarks. The Senate will then proceed to an Executive Session to resume consideration of Cal. #15, Allison Jones Rushing to be United States Circuit Judge for the Fourth Circuit. The Senate will vote on the Motion to Invoke Cloture on the Rushing nomination at 5:30 PM.
    5:30 pm
    The Senate convened and proceeded to Leader remarks.
      5:30 pm
      The Senate proceeded to an Executive Session.
      • Cal. #15, Allison Jones Rushing to be United States Circuit Judge for the Fourth Circuit.
      • Cal. #15, Allison Jones Rushing to be United States Circuit Judge for the Fourth Circuit. Motion to Invoke Cloture. Yeas and Nays ordered. The Motion was agreed to by a vote of 52–43.
      7:31 pm
      The Senate proceeded to a Period of Morning Business.
      • H.R. 1112 (Enhanced Background Checks Act of 2019). The bill was read for the first time. Further readings of the bill were objected to and the bill was placed on the calendar.
      • S. 617 (A bill to amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend certain expiring provisions, to provide disaster tax relief, and for other purposes). The bill was read for the second time. Further readings of the bill were objected to and the bill was placed on the calendar.
      • S. 252 (bill to authorize the honorary appointment of Robert J. Dole to the grade of colonel in the regular Army). The bill was passed by Unanimous Consent.
      • S. Res. 92 (Designating the first week of April 2019 as National Asbestos Awareness Week). The resolution was agreed to by Voice Vote.
      • H.R. 8 (Bipartisan Background Checks Act of 2019). The bill was read for the second time. Further readings of the bill were objected to and the bill was placed on the calendar.
      7:31 pm
      The Senate will convene at 10:00 AM on Tuesday, March 5, 2019. The Senate will then proceed to an Executive Session to resume consideration of Cal. #15, Allison Jones Rushing to be United States Circuit Judge for the Fourth Circuit. The Senate will recess from 12:30 PM – 2:15 PM for Weekly Conference Meetings.
