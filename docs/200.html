9:30 am
The Senate will convene at 9:30 AM and will proceed to an Executive Session to resume consideration of Cal. #18, Eric Murphy to be U.S. Circuit Judge for the Sixth Circuit. At 12:30 PM, all post-cloture time will be considered expired, and the Senate will vote on confirmation of the Murphy nomination. The Senate will then proceed to Cal. #11, John Fleming to be Assistant Secretary of Commerce for Economic Development, and the cloture motion will be withdrawn. At 1:45 PM, the Senate will vote on confirmation of the Fleming nomination.
    10:30 am
    The Senate convened and proceeded to an Executive Session.
    • Cal. #18, Eric Murphy to be U.S. Circuit Judge for the Sixth Circuit.
    • Cal. #18, Eric Murphy to be U.S. Circuit Judge for the Sixth Circuit. All post-cloture time was considered expired. Yeas and nays ordered.
    • Cal. #18, Eric Murphy to be U.S. Circuit Judge for the Sixth Circuit. All post-cloture time was considered expired. Yeas and nays ordered. The nomination was confirmed by a vote of 52-46.
    • Cal. #11, John Fleming to be Assistant Secretary of Commerce for Economic Development. The Motion to Invoke Cloture was withdrawn by Unanimous Consent. Yeas and nays ordered.
    • Cal. #11, John Fleming to be Assistant Secretary of Commerce for Economic Development. The Motion to Invoke Cloture was withdrawn by Unanimous Consent. Yeas and nays ordered. The nomination is confirmation by a vote of 67-30.
    • Motion to Proceed to a Legislative Session. Agreed to by Voice Vote.
    • Motion to Proceed to an Executive Session. Agreed to by Voice Vote.
    • Cal. #19, Paul Matey to be U.S. Circuit Judge for the Third Circuit. Sen. McConnell filed a Motion to Invoke Cloture on the nomination.
    • Cal. #107, Neomi J. Rao to be U.S. Circuit Judge for the District of Columbia. Sen. McConnell filed a Motion to Invoke Cloture on the nomination.
