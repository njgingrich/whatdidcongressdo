The Senate will convene at 9:30 AM and will proceed to Leader remarks. The Senate will then proceed to an Executive Session to resume the consideration of Cal. #788, Mike Pompeo to be Secretary of State. At 12:00 Noon, the Senate will vote on the Motion to Invoke Cloture on Cal. #788, Mike Pompeo to be Secretary of State. If cloture is invoked, all post-cloture time will be yielded back and the Senate will vote on the Grenell nomination.
