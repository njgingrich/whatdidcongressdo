9:30 am
The Senate will convene at 9:30 AM on Thursday, May 17, 2018. Following Leader remarks, Senator Paul will be recognized to make a Motion to Proceed to S. Con. Res. 36 (A concurrent resolution setting forth the congressional budget for the United States Government for fiscal year 2019 and setting forth the appropriate budgetary levels for fiscal years 2020 through 2028). There will be 90 minutes of debate. At approximately 11:15 AM, the Senate will vote on the Motion to Proceed to S. Con. Res. 36.
