9:30 am
The Senate will convene at 10:00 AM. Following Leader remarks, the Senate will proceed to Executive Session to resume consideration of Executive Calendar #1042, Joseph Maguire, of Florida, to be Director of the National Counterterrorism Center, Office of the Director of National Intelligence. At 11:00 AM, the Senate will vote on the Motion to Invoke Cloture on the Maguire nomination.
